Analyze this topic from multiple expert perspectives, then synthesize:

**Perspectives to consider:**
1. **Technical Expert**: What are the practical/technical realities?
2. **Skeptic**: What are the weaknesses, risks, and counterarguments?
3. **Optimist**: What are the opportunities and potential upsides?
4. **Historian**: What precedents and patterns exist?
5. **Futurist**: Where might this lead in 5-10 years?

**For each perspective, provide:**
- Key observations
- Evidence/reasoning
- Concerns or enthusiasm

**Then synthesize:**
- Points of agreement (high confidence)
- Significant disagreements
- Overall assessment
- Recommendations
